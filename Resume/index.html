Chyanna Lukander

My name is Chyanna Lukander and I've been an artist for over 25 years. I am entirely self-taught, though I am currently sharpening my skills through an official education at Seminole State College. I have experience in multiple mediums in motion graphics with a hint of performance experience, and I desire to utilize my knowledge and talents to create content that tells stories to be shared. My goal is not wealth or fame, but to connect with the world and hopefully inspire it through the passion and t.l.c. behind my work.
