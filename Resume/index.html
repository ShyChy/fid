Chyanna Lukander

Digital Artist

My name is Chyanna Lukander and I've been an artist for over 25 years. I am almost entirely self-taught, and sharpening my skills through education at Seminole State College.

I have experience in multiple mediums in motion graphics with a splash of performance experience, and I desire to utilize my knowledge and talents to create content that tells stories. My goal is not wealth or fame, but to connect with the world and inspire it through the passion and T.L.C. behind my work.
